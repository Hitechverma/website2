Vigya ITI

The Objective of the industry is to provide a facility to train and and prepare candidates industry ready with all the things capabilities that a industry ask from a prospective candidate.
The courses in ITI is designed to enhance the candidate's skill. The duration of the course may vary from one year to three years depending upon the trade opted by the candidate. At the end of the course the candidate will be a specialist in there opted field and will be eligible to work in the industry.
